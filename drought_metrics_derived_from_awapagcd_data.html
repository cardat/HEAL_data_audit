abstract Drought metrics data derived from AWAP and AGCD. Various drought indices have been computed as part of this dataset, offering a range of metrics for assessing drought conditions. This dataset provides a comprehensive foundation for investigating potential health implications of drought events as no specific drought index has been identified as directly tailored to health-related analyses.
